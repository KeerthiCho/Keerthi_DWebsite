About Us

At Siva Milk Union, Vijayawada, we are dedicated to bringing the farm’s freshness directly to your home. Our mission is simple: to provide pure, high-quality dairy products that families can trust.

Every item in our range is produced with care, combining traditional methods with modern hygiene standards to ensure the highest quality. We work closely with local farmers who share our commitment to sustainability, animal welfare, and ethical practices.

Our promise is purity. No preservatives, no additives, no shortcuts. Just wholesome, natural dairy products that reflect the goodness of nature every single day.
